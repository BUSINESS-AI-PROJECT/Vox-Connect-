Vox Connect AI

Autonomous Triage Machine for Clinics & Community Health Centers

🔍 Problem

Clinics face long queues, overwhelmed staff, and delays in diagnosis. Patients are often misdirected or misunderstood, reducing care quality.

💡 Solution

Vox Connect AI is a standalone machine that uses NLP and machine learning to collect symptoms, predict illness, and guide patients to the correct room — without human supervision.

🎯 Features

  • Touchscreen and voice input
  • Multilingual support (English, isiZulu, Afrikaans)
  • Offline operation on Raspberry Pi or Jetson Nano
  • Machine learning-based illness prediction
  • Referral guidance to correct department

📈 Impact

  • Reduced wait times
  • Improved triage accuracy
  • Inclusive access for diverse populations
  • Scalable across clinics and municipal centers
